About Us

Sri Vasavi Engineering College (SVEC) is a private engineering college located in Tadepalligudem, Andhra Pradesh, India. The college was established in 2001 by the Sri Vasavi Educational Society. SVEC is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Kakinada and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering. The college has a sprawling campus of over 100 acres. The campus is well-equipped with modern facilities, including a library, laboratories, workshops, and hostels. SVEC also has a strong alumni network that provides support to its students and alumni. SVEC is committed to providing quality education to its students. The college has a highly qualified faculty and offers a variety of extracurricular activities to its students. SVEC has been ranked among the top engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh by various publications. The college has a strong placement record. In the past year, SVEC students have been placed in top companies such as Infosys, TCS, and Wipro.
